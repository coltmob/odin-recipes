Salad Pizza

Abbot's Pizza Company's Famous Salad Pizza

Abbot Pizza Company is admired by locals, but for the rest of us, this neighborhood joint is pretty unheard of. With fan favorites like the Five Onion made with five different onions, three cheeses, and olive pestos sauce to Popeye's Chicken made with tequila lime marinated chicken, cheese, veggies and garlic pesto - sticking to your New Year's diet can be tricky. (Did we mention that Abbot's crusts come in bagel-like varieties? Choose between onion, poppy, garlic, hot pepper, sesame, and everything!)

What's inside? Mozzarella Cheese and Onion Baked Crust, A Thin Layer Of Sour Cream, Then Topped With A Healthy Layer Of Chopped Salad, Feta Cheese And Tomatoes Sprinkled On Top, Drizzled With Our House Lemon-Olive Oil Dressing, Covered With A Layer Of Fresh Avocados

Ingredients

  • 4 oz Mozzarella cheese
  • One medium onion
  • One roma tomato
  • One lemon
  • One avocado
  • Pre-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Favorite salad mix
  • Handful of feta cheese
  • 3 tbs Olive oil
  • Handful of poppy seed, sesame seeds, and about four chopped garlic cloves
  • Two eggs
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 425F
  2. Prepare Ingredients
    • Shred Mozzarella
    • Slice Onion
    • Dice Tomato
    • Slice Avocado
    • Halve lemon
    • Mix together poppy seeds, sesame seeds, and chopped garlic
    • Wisk eggs in a bowl
  3. Roll out pizza dough
  4. Layer pizza dough first with olive oil, then mozzarella and onions
  5. Brush crust with egg wash and sprinkle on poppyseed, sesame, garlic mix
  6. Cook pizza in oven for about 15min or until done to your liking
  7. Meanwhile, toss salad mix with a dash of oil, lemon juice from a lemon half, diced tomato, feta cheese, salt, and pepper
  8. Remove pizza and immediately top with a generous layer of sour cream
  9. Finish pizza first with the prepared salad mix, then the avocado
  10. Feel free to add more feta or lemon!
